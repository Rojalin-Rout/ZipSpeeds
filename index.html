Frequently Asked Questions

For Customers

What is Zipspeeds?

Zipspeeds is a fast, reliable, and affordable logistics service that offers on-demand Intra-city, Last mile Logistics delivery solutions for businesses and individuals across Bhubaneswar & Cuttack.

What kind of deliveries can I book on Zipspeeds?

You can book deliveries for parcels, food, medicines, groceries, electronics, and furniture, Bulk items & more – anything legal and safe to transport within the city.

How do I book a delivery?

You can book a delivery through:
• WhatsApp (Direct booking via chat)
• Our Website (booking form)
• Call support (available 7 AM – 8 PM daily)

How much does a delivery cost?

Delivery charges depend on distance, weight, and urgency. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. For bulk or regular deliveries, we offer special business rates.

How can I track my delivery?

Once your delivery is picked up, you'll receive a live tracking link via WhatsApp or SMS to track the driver in real-time.

How fast is the delivery?

We offer:
• Standard Delivery (within 2-3 hours)
• Express Delivery (within 1 hour, extra charges apply)

What if my package gets lost or damaged?

All packages are handled with care. In rare cases of damage/loss, our team will investigate and provide compensation as per our terms & conditions.

Do you offer bulk delivery services for businesses?

Yes! We specialize in B2B logistics. We offer regular pickups, dashboard access, dedicated relationship managers, and volume discounts for businesses.

What are your delivery timings?

We operate 7 days a week from 7 AM to 10 PM. For special cases or night deliveries, contact our support team.

What areas do you serve?

Currently, we operate across Bhubaneswar city. We are expanding soon to nearby cities.

For Driver Partners

How can I join Zipspeeds as a delivery partner?

Simple! Just contact us via WhatsApp or call, and submit:
• Aadhaar card
• Driving license
• Vehicle RC
• Bank account details
After verification, you'll be onboarded and trained.

What are the benefits of working with Zipspeeds?

• Flexible working hours
• Guaranteed daily payouts
• Incentives & bonuses
• Supportive team
• Respectful work culture

What kind of vehicle is required?

You can join with a bike, scooty, 3w commercial auto, Tata Ace, pickup truck, 407 (for short distance deliveries). We also support tempo and van drivers for bulk goods.

How are delivery partners paid?

You'll be paid per delivery, with weekly or daily payout options. Extra incentives for high performance or long-distance orders.

Do I need a smartphone?

Yes, a basic Android smartphone is needed for receiving orders, navigation, and communication.

Is there any joining fee?

No joining fee at all. We believe in empowering riders, not charging them.

What if a customer misbehaves or cancels the order?

Our team provides full support to driver-partners in such situations. We ensure your safety and fair treatment always.
